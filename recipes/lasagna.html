Lasagna Recipe

Description

Really nice lasagna dish that makes you feel like youre eating at moms. Made with only the freshest ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Can of corn
  1. STEP 1 To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. STEP 2 Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the meat mixture.
  3. STEP 3 Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce recipe and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season.
  4. STEP 4 Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.
  5. STEP 5 Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).
  6. STEP 6 Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade white sauce.
  7. STEP 7 Repeat until you have three layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you cant see any pasta poking through.
  8. STEP 8 Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.
  9. STEP 9 Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
